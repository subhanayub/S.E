The Day We Met

"Har saal aata hai, lekin is saal ki shuruaat alag hai, kyunki is saal mere paas aap ho, meri duaon ka jawab ho. 6th June ko Allah ne humein milwaya, us din meri zindagi mein ek roshni ka ujaala aaya—jaise ice cream ke pehle bite mein dil khil jata hai."

Whispers of the Heart

"Phir kuch din hum khamosh rahe, lekin dil kabhi chup na raha. Har pal aap ka intezaar, aap ki yaad dil ke paas raha. Jab milenge hum, baatein karte-karte door nikle jayenge, aur main sirf aapko dekhoon—jaise ice cream pighalta hai, waise hi dil bhi."

Our Forever Began

"November aaya, aur humne dil ke raaz khol diye. Mohabbat ka izhaar kiya, aur Alhamdulillah, hum saath ho gaye. You walked into my life like a gentle breeze, melting my heart like ice cream on a warm day, and with you, I found my sweetest forever."

My Unseen Love

"Eu te amei de longe—I loved you from afar, jaise ice cream ke bina bhi uski thandak mehsoos hoti hai. Sem o seu cheiro, sem o seu abraço, sem tocar—phir bhi tum meri zindagi ka woh mithaas ho jo har gham ko pighla deti hai."

My Eternal Vow

"Jab milenge hum, baatein karte-karte door nikle jayenge, aur main sirf aapko dekhoon—tum meri ice cream ho, jo har pal ko mithaas se bhar deti ho. Ab naye saal ka har din tumhare saath guzarne ki dua hai, chahe saari duniya khilaaf ho, mera dil sirf tumhara hai. Happy New Year, meri zindagi!"

"Main bura hoon—main. Agar maine kabhi koi galti ki, maaf kar dena. Main perfect nahi, par tumhe apna sab kuch dena chahta hoon—jaise ice cream ka akhiri bite bhi tumhare liye chhod doon. Aap mera sab kuch ho."
